Chrome Android — Popup Blocker Bypass

How it works: On Android Chrome, the popup blocker dialog shows URLs truncated to ~45 characters. The @evil.com falls in the "..." truncation zone, making the spoofed URL look identical to the legitimate Google URL.

Both URLs show: www.google.com/...search?q=what+is+claude
